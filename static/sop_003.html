✅ SOP-003: Vendor Compliance Checks

Standard Operating Procedure for Vendor Compliance Verification

Purpose

To ensure all vendor deliveries meet company requirements for documentation, packaging, labeling, and quality standards before acceptance.

Pre-Delivery Requirements

Documentation Verification

  • Valid purchase order number
  • Packing slip with itemized details
  • Certificate of conformance (if required)
  • Material safety data sheets (MSDS) for hazardous materials
  • Country of origin documentation

Packaging Standards

  • Pallets in good condition (Grade A or better)
  • Proper stretch wrap application
  • Maximum pallet height restrictions observed
  • No overhang beyond pallet edges
  • Stable stacking pattern

Labeling Requirements

  • Clear and legible product identification
  • Quantity and unit of measure visible
  • Lot numbers and expiration dates (if applicable)
  • Hazmat labels for dangerous goods
  • Company-specific label requirements met

Compliance Checklist

Delivery Time Compliance

  • Delivery within scheduled appointment window
  • Advance notice provided for any delays
  • Driver arrives with proper identification
  • Vehicle condition meets safety standards

Product Compliance

  • Items match purchase order specifications
  • Quantities are accurate
  • Product condition meets quality standards
  • No substitutions without prior approval

Environmental Compliance

  • Temperature-sensitive items properly maintained
  • No contamination or pest issues
  • Proper segregation of incompatible materials
  • Environmental controls documented

Non-Compliance Actions

Minor Non-Compliance

  • Document issues in QR Legends system
  • Accept delivery with conditions
  • Send corrective action request to vendor
  • Schedule follow-up verification

Major Non-Compliance

  • Reject delivery immediately
  • Document all non-compliance issues
  • Notify purchasing and vendor immediately
  • Initiate supplier corrective action process
  • Consider vendor rating impact

Documentation Requirements

  • Use QR Legends app to scan all compliance checks
  • Photograph any non-compliance issues
  • Complete vendor scorecard entries
  • Generate compliance reports monthly
  • Maintain audit trail for all decisions
