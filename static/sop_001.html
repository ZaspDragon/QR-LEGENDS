📦 SOP-001: Inbound Freight Processing

Standard Operating Procedure for Processing Incoming Freight

Purpose

To establish standardized procedures for receiving, inspecting, and processing inbound freight to ensure accuracy, efficiency, and compliance with company standards.

Scope

This procedure applies to all warehouse personnel involved in receiving inbound freight from suppliers, carriers, and third-party logistics providers.

Procedure Steps

Step 1: Pre-Arrival Preparation

  • Review delivery schedule and expected shipments
  • Verify dock door assignments
  • Ensure receiving area is clear and organized
  • Check that necessary equipment is available (forklifts, scanners, etc.)

Step 2: Driver Check-In

  • Verify driver identification and delivery documentation
  • Direct driver to appropriate dock door
  • Log arrival time and trailer number
  • Conduct safety briefing if required

Step 3: Documentation Review

  • Compare delivery paperwork with purchase orders
  • Verify quantities, part numbers, and descriptions
  • Note any discrepancies for investigation
  • Scan QR codes on delivery documents using QR Legends app

Step 4: Physical Inspection

  • Inspect exterior condition of packages and pallets
  • Note any visible damage or defects
  • Count quantities and verify against documentation
  • Take photos of any damaged items

Step 5: System Processing

  • Scan received items using QR Legends mobile app
  • Update inventory system with received quantities
  • Generate and print location labels
  • Create exception reports for discrepancies

Step 6: Put-Away Preparation

  • Stage items in designated receiving area
  • Assign put-away locations based on item characteristics
  • Generate put-away tasks in WMS
  • Communicate priority items to put-away team

Required Equipment

  • QR Legends mobile scanner/device
  • Forklift or pallet jack
  • Label printer
  • Measuring tape
  • Camera for damage documentation

Safety Requirements

  • Wear appropriate PPE (hard hat, safety vest, steel-toed boots)
  • Follow lockout/tagout procedures for equipment
  • Maintain clear walkways and exits
  • Report unsafe conditions immediately

Quality Standards

  • 99.5% receiving accuracy target
  • All discrepancies documented within 2 hours
  • Damaged items quarantined immediately
  • Complete processing within 4 hours of arrival
