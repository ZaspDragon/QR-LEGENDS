🦺 Safety Protocols

Warehouse safety procedures, emergency protocols, training, and incident reporting.

🚨 Emergency Procedures

Use this section for fire evacuation, medical emergencies, severe weather, and hazmat spills.

  • Stop work and alert a supervisor immediately.
  • Call 911 for life-threatening injuries, fire, or uncontrolled hazards.
  • Use the nearest safe exit and report to the muster point.
  • Do not re-enter until management or emergency responders clear the area.
⚡ Equipment Safety

Forklift, lifting, dock, and machinery safety reminders.

  • Complete equipment inspection before use.
  • Wear required PPE and keep aisles clear.
  • Sound horn at intersections and blind corners.
  • Tag out unsafe equipment and report it before reuse.
🎯 Safety Training

Track required training and refresher topics.

  • Forklift certification and evaluations.
  • Hazard communication and PPE training.
  • Emergency action plan review.
  • Near-miss reporting expectations.
📋 Incident Reporting

Use this for injuries, property damage, unsafe conditions, and near misses.

  • Record date, time, location, people involved, and what happened.
  • Take photos if safe to do so.
  • Notify supervisor immediately.
  • Follow up with corrective action before closing the incident.
