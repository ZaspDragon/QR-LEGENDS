🚚 SOP-202: Carrier Selection Guidelines

Standard Operating Procedure for Selecting Optimal Shipping Carriers

Purpose

To ensure optimal carrier selection based on cost, service level, delivery requirements, and customer preferences.

Primary Selection Criteria

Delivery Requirements

  • Customer-requested delivery date
  • Service level commitments (next day, 2-day, ground)
  • Geographic destination and accessibility
  • Special handling requirements

Cost Considerations

  • Base shipping rate comparison
  • Dimensional weight vs. actual weight pricing
  • Fuel surcharges and additional fees
  • Volume discount qualification

Service Reliability

  • On-time delivery performance by carrier
  • Damage rates and claims history
  • Tracking and communication capabilities
  • Customer service responsiveness

Carrier Selection Matrix

Express/Overnight Shipments

  • Priority: FedEx Express, UPS Next Day Air
  • Use for urgent orders or customer requests
  • Required for time-sensitive materials
  • Automatic signature confirmation for high-value items

Standard Ground Shipments

  • Priority: UPS Ground, FedEx Ground, USPS Priority
  • Use rate shopping to select best price
  • Consider delivery time to destination zone
  • Factor in pickup schedules and cutoff times

Freight Shipments (Over 150 lbs)

  • LTL carriers: XPO, Old Dominion, FedEx Freight
  • Compare accessorial charges
  • Verify liftgate and inside delivery options
  • Check transit time commitments

Special Handling Requirements

Hazardous Materials

  • Use certified hazmat carriers only
  • Verify current certifications and training
  • Follow IATA/DOT regulations strictly
  • Document all hazmat shipment details

Temperature-Controlled Items

  • Select carriers with cold chain capabilities
  • Verify temperature monitoring during transit
  • Use expedited service to minimize exposure time
  • Require signature and immediate delivery

High-Value Shipments

  • Use carriers with enhanced security features
  • Require signature confirmation
  • Purchase additional insurance coverage
  • Track shipments proactively

Customer Preferences

Account-Specific Requirements

  • Check customer shipping preferences in system
  • Use customer's preferred carrier when specified
  • Honor customer pickup instructions
  • Apply customer shipping account numbers when provided

Restricted Carriers

  • Note any carriers prohibited by customer
  • Verify delivery address accessibility
  • Check for residential vs. commercial delivery
  • Confirm any special delivery instructions

Decision Documentation

  • Record carrier selection rationale in QR Legends system
  • Note any special circumstances affecting choice
  • Track actual performance vs. promised service
  • Update carrier performance metrics regularly
