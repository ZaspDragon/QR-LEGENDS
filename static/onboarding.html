System Overview

QR Legends streamlines your warehouse operations with these key features:

🔍 QR Code Technology

Every item gets a unique, permanent QR code that never changes. Scan to track, move, and manage inventory in real-time.

📱 Mobile-First Design

Use any smartphone or tablet to scan items, update inventory, and manage operations from anywhere in your warehouse.

📊 Real-Time Analytics

Track performance, monitor accuracy, and optimize workflows with comprehensive reporting and analytics.

Hold device steady for 1-2 seconds
