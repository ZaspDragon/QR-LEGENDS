🔍 SOP-002: Quality Inspection Standards

Standard Operating Procedure for Quality Control Inspections

Purpose

To establish consistent quality inspection standards for all incoming and outgoing products to ensure customer satisfaction and regulatory compliance.

Inspection Types

Incoming Inspection

  • Visual inspection for damage, defects, or contamination
  • Dimensional verification for critical specifications
  • Functional testing where applicable
  • Documentation review and compliance check

In-Process Inspection

  • Random sampling during handling and storage
  • Condition monitoring for environmental factors
  • Packaging integrity verification
  • Location accuracy confirmation

Outgoing Inspection

  • Final quality check before shipment
  • Packaging and labeling verification
  • Order accuracy confirmation
  • Customer-specific requirements validation

Inspection Criteria

Accept Criteria

  • No visible damage or defects
  • All specifications within tolerance
  • Complete documentation package
  • Proper packaging and labeling

Reject Criteria

  • Physical damage affecting functionality
  • Missing or incorrect documentation
  • Contamination or foreign materials
  • Dimensional non-conformance

Hold Criteria

  • Minor defects requiring evaluation
  • Questionable specifications
  • Incomplete documentation pending resolution
  • Customer notification required

Documentation Requirements

  • Use QR Legends app to log all inspection results
  • Photograph any defects or non-conformances
  • Complete inspection checklists for each item type
  • Generate quality certificates as required
  • Maintain traceability records

Corrective Actions

  • Immediately quarantine non-conforming items
  • Notify supervisor and quality manager
  • Document root cause when identified
  • Implement preventive measures
  • Follow up on supplier notifications
